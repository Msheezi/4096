How to play

  1. Use the arrow keys to move the tiles in the desired direction
  2. Tiles combine when identical values collide and their value will increase
  3. Continue combining tiles until your tile value reaches 4096
  4. After each move, a new tile is placed in a random position on the board
  5. The game ends when you reach 4096 or there are no more available moves
